Music/Writing

I was told I had been singing since the early age of two. It was a peaceful way to express my emotions without speaking, even though you are singing words. As I got older in life, singing never slowed down for me. I began writing my own music at age 13. I improved my writing as I got older and my ability to incorporate it into my own personal songs. By 17 years old I attended a charter school for vocals. In this school I was ecouraged to learn piano. Once I learned piano, the sky was the limit. I could not get away from music and writing. Finally, I began to play my own songs on the piano while singing to my hearts content. It was safety for me. It was my heaven.

Today, music is still a huge part of my everyday life. After releasing my first single in 2018, "Kill Me One Last Time", I stopped playing piano. However, I never stopped writing.
